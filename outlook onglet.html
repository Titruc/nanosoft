Microsoft Outlook is a widely used email and calendar client known for its integration with Microsoft 365 and strong organizational tools. It’s popular in professional environments but can feel heavy for casual users. Pros: powerful email management, excellent calendar integration, great for business, works seamlessly with Microsoft services. Cons: can be slow or resource-heavy, cluttered interface for beginners, some features locked behind subscriptions. Fun fact: Outlook’s first version dates back to 1997, originally built as a simple scheduler before becoming a full email suite.
